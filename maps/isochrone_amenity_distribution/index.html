This map displays various locations that can be reached within a specific travel time, known as an isochrone. These places are categorized based on the type of activities they primarily host: social, optional, or necessary, following the classification by Gehl. The travel-time isochrones are generated using the openrouteservice API. The place data is sourced from the Overture Maps release dated October 19, 2023 (places theme, confidence > 0.6).
